About Me and This Website

Educated Gaming was made because, as a child, I often found myself playing video games more than watching movies or reading books. As a result, the stories and lessons I learned from games have been invaluable. In spite of this, many people do not believe video games can tell worthwhile stories or provide useful knowledge. This website is here to prove them wrong.
